Urbania Background

In the ever-expanding urban landscapes, where green spaces are often limited, Urbania emerges as a revolutionary app designed to bring the benefits of urban farming to individuals, communities, and cities at large. This innovative platform seamlessly blends digital learning and gamification, inspiring users to become urban farmers while fostering a sense of community and sustainable living.

Mission

    To be the hub of Center for Urban Agriculture and Innovation initiatives to achieve the sustainable development goals.

Vision

To be recognized as the leader in Urban Agriculture and Innovation.

Behind Urbania

This project was developed as part of a capstone team building mobile app. The app aimed to facilitate collaboration and communication among team members, providing tools and features to enhance productivity and project management. Through this project, team members learned valuable skills in mobile app development, teamwork, and project coordination.
